Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

What QRSmith does not collect

QRSmith generates QR codes entirely in your browser. The URL, text, WiFi details, contact card, or email you enter, any logo image you upload, and the QR code itself are processed locally on your device using JavaScript. None of that data is ever sent to, stored on, or logged by any server we operate.

Hosting and analytics

This site is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub's infrastructure may log standard web server data (such as IP address and request timing) for security and operational purposes, governed byGitHub's own privacy statement. We do not run any additional tracking scripts on top of that.

Advertising

If this site displays advertising (for example, through Google AdSense), the ad provider may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads and measure performance. You can control ad personalization through your browser settings or viaGoogle's Ads Settings.

Cookies

QRSmith itself does not set any cookies. Any cookies present on this site come from hosting infrastructure or advertising partners, not from the QR generation tool.

Questions about this policy can be sent tohello@qrsmith.app.
