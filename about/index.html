About QRSmith

QRSmith does one thing: it turns a URL, a WiFi network, a contact card, an email, or any text into a styled, scannable QR code, with your logo or initials embedded in the center if you want them. That's it. No account, no dashboard, no upsell funnel.

We built it as a single-purpose tool on purpose. Most QR generators are buried inside a bloated "all-in-one" utility site with dozens of unrelated tools competing for space. QRSmith is just the QR generator — fast to load, easy to find, and easy to trust.

Everything happens client-side, in your browser, using the open-source qr-code-stylinglibrary. The text you type, the image you upload, and the QR code it produces never touch a server. Nothing is stored, logged, or sent anywhere.

QR codes have built-in error correction, which is what makes it safe to place a logo over the middle of the code — as long as the logo doesn't cover too much of the pattern, the code still scans reliably. QRSmith uses high error correction automatically whenever a logo is present.
